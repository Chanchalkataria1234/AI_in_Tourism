==============================
UDAIPUR KNOWLEDGE BASE
==============================

[CHUNK ABOUT]
Udaipur is a historic city in Rajasthan, India, known as the "City of Lakes". It is famous for its royal palaces, scenic lakes, cultural heritage, and romantic atmosphere.

[CHUNK RESORT]
Meera Valley Resort is located near Udaipur.
Facilities:
- Swimming Pool
- Garden
- Trekking Area
- Game Zone

[CHUNK BEST TIME]
The best time to visit Udaipur is from October to March when the weather is pleasant and suitable for sightseeing.

[CHUNK TRANSPORT]
- Nearest Airport: Maharana Pratap Airport (20–25 km from city)
- Nearest Railway Station: Udaipur City Railway Station
- Local transport: Auto rickshaws, taxis, rental bikes, and private cabs

==============================
TOP ATTRACTIONS
==============================

[CHUNK CITY PALACE]
City Palace is the largest palace complex in Rajasthan, located on the banks of Lake Pichola. It showcases royal architecture, museums, courtyards, and panoramic views of the lake.

[CHUNK LAKE PICHOLA]
Lake Pichola is an artificial lake famous for boat rides and scenic sunset views. It features attractions like Jag Mandir and Lake Palace.

[CHUNK FATEH SAGAR]
Fateh Sagar Lake is a popular lake surrounded by hills. It is known for boating, evening walks, and local street food.

[CHUNK SAJJANGARH]
Sajjangarh Monsoon Palace is located on a hilltop and offers breathtaking sunset views over Udaipur. It is a popular viewpoint.

[CHUNK SAHELIYON KI BARI]
Saheliyon Ki Bari is a historic garden with fountains, lotus pools, marble pavilions, and greenery. It was built for royal ladies.

[CHUNK JAGDISH TEMPLE]
Jagdish Temple is a famous Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Vishnu, located near City Palace. It is known for intricate carvings.

[CHUNK BAGORE KI HAVELI]
Bagore Ki Haveli is a historic haveli located near Lake Pichola. It hosts evening cultural shows including traditional dance and music.

[CHUNK SHILPGRAM]
Shilpgram is a rural arts and crafts complex showcasing traditional Rajasthani culture, handicrafts, and village life.

==============================
SPECIAL PLACES
==============================

[CHUNK NEEMACH MATA]
Neemach Mata Temple is a hilltop temple near Fateh Sagar Lake. It is popular for sunrise and sunset views and requires a short trek or drive.

==============================
ACTIVITIES
==============================

[CHUNK BOATING]
Boating is a popular activity in Lake Pichola and Fateh Sagar Lake, especially during sunset.

[CHUNK SHOPPING]
Popular shopping areas include Hathi Pol Market, Bada Bazaar, and local street markets for handicrafts, paintings, and souvenirs.

[CHUNK CULTURAL]
Cultural activities include folk dance shows at Bagore Ki Haveli and visiting heritage sites.

==============================
FOOD
==============================

[CHUNK FOOD]
Udaipur offers traditional Rajasthani cuisine such as Dal Baati Churma, Gatte Ki Sabzi, and local sweets. Street food is also popular near lakes and markets.

==============================
TRAVEL TIPS
==============================

[CHUNK TIPS]
- Start sightseeing early to avoid crowds
- Carry water during summer
- Wear comfortable shoes for walking
- Book tickets in advance for major attractions
- Sunset points are very popular
- Always start the day in morning and end the day at night in meera valley resort
- Balance sightseeing and relaxation
