Explore Udaipur

Attractions Near
the Resort

Udaipur — the Venice of the East — is adorned with magnificent palaces, serene lakes, and centuries of Mewar heritage. Every corner tells a story.

Palace
City Palace
A majestic complex of palaces built over 400 years by the Maharanas of Mewar. Offers stunning views of Lake Pichola and the Aravalli hills.
Fort
Sajjangarh
The Monsoon Palace perched atop the Aravalli hills, offering breathtaking panoramic views of Udaipur, especially magical at sunset.
Lake
Lake Pichola
An enchanting artificial lake created in 1362 AD, home to the iconic Jag Mandir island palace and unforgettable sunset boat rides.
Gardens
Saheliyon ki Bari
The Garden of Maidens — a royal retreat with ornate fountains, kiosks, marble elephants, and a lotus pool built for royal ladies.
